CAMERAS

Webcam

These are used for capturing photos and videos on a computer but more commonly to connect to the internet to video chat.

Home Security Camera

Home security cameras usually use the internet to connect to a phone or computer to stream the live footage and store it. Multiple cameras can be connected to a network to supply more than one perspective.

CCTV

Closed circuit television video surveillance cameras on the same network transmit a signal to the same place to be viewed.

TELEVISION

Aerial

Aerials receive television signals transmitted through the air. Aerials create a network through transmitters and receivers to pass information.

Satelite Dish

Satelite dishes also receive signals and turn them into an electical signal for a TV.

EFTPOS

ATM Machine

ATM's access the network that holds your banking account information.

Eftpos

EFTPOS machines access the EFTPOS network that processes payments to the bank.

Mobile Payment Device

This device also connects to the banking network but accepts payments through a smartphone.

POWER

Outlet

This is a single faze power outlet in the street that allows people to connect to the power grid in the city.

Power Pole

Power poles connect the cables in the power network from house to house. They also connect houses to internet and link wires for the street lighting network.

Switch/Sub Board

This is the point of control for the power in a building, a point in the power network where you can do maintenance and turn off the power.

Power Mains

This is the mains power entry point in a building for a high voltage input. This connects the building to the power grid.

SECURITY

Alarm

This box represents the security system in the building. It has a flashing light, alarm and back up battery. The alarm system connects into a security network where the alarms can be monitered and possible surveillance footage can be sent.
